Product Introduction

Add a brand new column to your SharePoint Email Column. This type of field can save you time from filling out the @domain information. For example, when support@hotmail.com is what you need to type in for this field, you only need to enter support and choose the support@hotmail.com from the options.

Creation

After you installed SharePoint Email Column, you are able to get new options in the field list. If you want to create a new field to hold email adresses in your list, all you need to do is choose this option.

Settings

On the Setting page, there are certain things you can do:
  1. Put the most desired email domains into a list. When end users are typing, they will get a list of options with these domain names.
  2. You can remove one item from this list by double-cliking on the item.
  3. By checking "Only the emails with above addresses are allowed" you can restrict the field value to the domain list.
  4. You can convert exsiting Single Line Text column into a Email column.

Domain Information Auto-completion

When end users are typing, the email field will show a list of emails with pre-defined domain names for you. You can choose the one you want by moving the focus with the "Up" and "Down" button on your keyboard.

Email Address Validation

By default, email column will validate the user's input. This validation guarantees only the email address in the right format can be saved.
