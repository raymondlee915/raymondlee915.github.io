SharePoint Email Column

Add a brand new column to your SharePoint, Email Column. The field in this type can save your time from filling the @domain information. For example, when support@hotmail.com is what you need to type in for this field, you only need to enter support and chose the support@hotmail.com from the options we prepared for you.

Creation

After you intalled SharePoint Email Column, you can get a new option in the field list. If you want to create a new field to hold email adresses in your list, all you need to do is choose this new type.

Settings

On the Setting page, there are certain things you can do:
  1. Put the most desired email domains into a list. When end users trying to type in they will get a list of options with these domain names.
  2. You can remove one item from this list by double-cliking on the item.
  3. By checking "Only the emails with above addresses are allowed" can help you restrict the field value to the domain list.
  4. You can convert exsiting Single Line Text column into a Email column.

Domain Information Auto-completion

When end users keep typing in, email field will show a list of emails with pre-defined domain names for you. You can choose the one you want by moving the focus with the "Up" and "Down" button on your keyboard.

Email Address Validation

By default, email column will validate the user's input. This validation guarantees only the email address in the right format can be saved.
