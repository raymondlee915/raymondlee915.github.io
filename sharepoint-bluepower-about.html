About Us

BluePower Solutions is a startup company organized by several experienced .NET developers with rich experience in Microsoft SharePoint Platform, and are dedicated to developing innovative web parts and solutions to expand Microsoft SharePoint’s out-of-box capabilities, maximize efficiency and enhance overall productivity.

Our team members have been working on Microsoft's SharePoint Platform for many years, and have developed dozens of products ranging from components and applications to solutions and suites for SharePoint users worldwide. We design products that answer unsolved business needs by out-of-the-box SharePoint, and give customers maximum benefit at minimum cost.

As a customer-oriented company, we make life easier for customers by delivering great products and services that are relevant and practical to your personal and business need and operations, and thus help customers to save time and money. We value customer feedback, and are willing to optimize our products based on user-based observations. We expect to build long lasting customer relationships with you.
